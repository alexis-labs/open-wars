# Open Wars audio licenses

## Sound effects (`ui/audio/sfx/`)

All files under `sfx/` are **Open Wars original sound effects**, authored for this project and released under **[CC0 1.0](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/)** (public domain).

They are baked from the procedural profiles in `proceduralProfiles.ts` via `pnpm audio:generate`. You may use, modify, and redistribute them without attribution.

## Music placeholders

Background music entries in `ui/Audio.tsx` still point at `Empty.ogg` / `Empty.aac` placeholders. Replacing them requires separately licensed tracks (not included in this repository).
